TiO2 films by a black dye based on a
trithiocyanato–ruthenium complex(Note: The full text of this document is currently only available in the PDF Version )Md. K. Nazeeruddin, P. Péchy and M. GrätzelAbstract
A black trithiocyanato–ruthenium(II) terpyridyl
complex where the terpyridyl ligand is substituted by three carboxyl
groups in 4,4′,4″-positions achieves very efficient
panchromatic sensitization of nanocrystalline TiO2 solar cells
over the whole visible range extending into the near-IR region up to 920
nm.
